At it's simplest, a subscriber attaches AJAX actions to a simple email subcribe form. It is triggered on 'submit', it reports errors when the email is not valid, and it calls a callback on AJAX completion with the contents returned.

At the end of <body>, <script> blocks include build/das.js and create the subscriber behavior. A <style> block is set in the <head> of this page to indicate loading and to hide the form .errors until needed.
